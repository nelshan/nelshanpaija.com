Background

I'm a Unity Game Developer with hands-on experience in bringing game worlds to life in both 2D and 3D. Currently based in Nepal, my journey began with a deep love for gaming, which eventually evolved into a passion for game development. I graduated from Informatics College Pokhara, which has a direct partnership with London Metropolitan University, UK, earning Second-Class Honours in BSc (Hons) Computing.

As an indie game developer, I focus on pushing the boundaries of creativity, leveraging my expertise in C# and the Unity Engine to bring innovative gameplay concepts to life. I have experience building diverse game prototypes and interactive experiences, and I take pride in crafting seamless user interfaces that enhance player engagement. While Unity is my primary tool, I believe in using the right tool for the job—constantly exploring new technologies and techniques to expand my skill set.

Whether I’m scripting complex gameplay mechanics, optimizing game performance, or designing visually appealing game environments, I’m committed to delivering high-quality results. I’m always seeking new opportunities to collaborate and grow, constantly honing my skills as a game developer.

If I'm not coding or working on personal projects, I'm probably participating in game jams, exploring local landscapes, or diving into the world of UI/UX design to improve player experiences
