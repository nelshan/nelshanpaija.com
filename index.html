I graduated from Informatics College Pokhara, which has a direct partnership with London Metropolitan University, UK, earning Second-Class Honours in BSc (Hons) Computing.

As a passionate indie Game Developer, I'm always excited to dive into new challenges and grow my skills. I specialize in C# and the Unity Engine, which I've used to create a variety of game prototypes and interactive experiences. While Unity is my primary tool, I believe in using the best tool for each project, and I'm always open to learning new technologies and techniques that can help bring my ideas to life. Whether it's scripting gameplay mechanics, optimizing performance, or designing engaging game environments, I'm committed to delivering the best results possible.
