LIGHTLESS – 2D Top-Down Survival Shooter

Role: Solo Developer | Designer | Programmer

Engine: Unity

Platform: PC (Web)

Play it on: Itch.io

Overview

LIGHTLESS is a minimalist 2D top-down shooter that immerses players in a relentless survival experience. Set in a world consumed by darkness, players must fend off endless waves of the undead, relying solely on a flickering light source and a single weapon. The game's core mechanic revolves around the interplay of light and darkness, creating a tense atmosphere where visibility is limited, and danger lurks in the shadows.

Key Features

  • Dynamic Lighting Mechanics: The player's light source is both a guide and a shield, illuminating the immediate surroundings while leaving the periphery shrouded in darkness, enhancing the game's suspense.
  • Endless Survival Mode: Face continuous waves of enemies, testing reflexes and strategic movement in a battle against time and overwhelming odds.
  • Intuitive Controls:
    • Movement: WASD keys
    • Aiming: Mouse
    • Shooting: Left Click

Development Highlights

  • Solo Development: Conceptualized, designed, and developed independently, showcasing proficiency in game design, programming, and asset integration.
  • Unity Engine Utilization: Leveraged Unity's capabilities to implement real-time lighting effects, enemy AI behaviors, and responsive controls.
  • Asset Management: Curated and integrated visual and audio assets to create an immersive and cohesive game environment.

Reflection

LIGHTLESS was an exploration into creating tension and atmosphere through minimalist design. The project emphasized the importance of lighting in gameplay mechanics and taught valuable lessons in balancing challenges with player engagement.
