Run from Unknown – First-Person Survival Horror

Role: Solo Developer | Designer | Programmer

Engine: Unity

Platform: PC (Web)

Play it on: Itch.io

Overview

Run from Unknown is a short, first-person survival horror game that immerses players in a tense and mysterious environment. Waking up alone in a dark, abandoned house, players must navigate through eerie corridors and solve puzzles to uncover the truth behind their predicament.

Key Features

  • Atmospheric Horror: Dark, abandoned house setting with eerie atmosphere.
  • Puzzle Elements: Environmental puzzles that reveal the story.
  • First-Person Exploration: Immersive exploration mechanics.
  • Story-Driven Gameplay: Narrative unfolds through exploration and discovery.

Controls

  • Movement: WASD
  • Look Around: Mouse
  • Interact: E key
  • Objective: Explore and solve puzzles to escape

Development Highlights

  • 3D Environment Design: Created immersive indoor environments.
  • Puzzle System: Implemented interactive puzzle mechanics.
  • Atmosphere Creation: Used lighting and sound for horror atmosphere.

Reflection

This project explored the fundamentals of 3D game development and atmospheric design. Creating tension through environmental storytelling was a valuable learning experience.
