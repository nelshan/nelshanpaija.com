Space Invaders – Classic Arcade Shooter

Role: Solo Developer | Designer | Programmer

Engine: Unity

Platform: PC (Web)

Play it on: Itch.io

Overview

Space Invader is a faithful recreation of the classic arcade shooter, developed using Unity and C#. Players control a lone starfighter, tasked with defending Earth against relentless waves of descending alien invaders.

Key Features

  • Classic Shooter Mechanics: Faithful recreation of the original Space Invaders gameplay.
  • Wave-Based Combat: Progressive difficulty with increasingly challenging alien formations.
  • Precise Controls: Responsive movement and shooting mechanics.
  • Score System: Track progress and compete for high scores.

Controls

  • Movement: Left/Right Arrow Keys or A/D
  • Shoot: Spacebar
  • Objective: Defeat all alien invaders

Development Highlights

  • Unity 2D Development: Implemented classic arcade shooter mechanics.
  • Enemy AI: Created alien movement patterns and behaviors.
  • Collision System: Precise bullet and enemy collision detection.

Reflection

This project reinforced the importance of tight controls and satisfying feedback in arcade games. The simple yet engaging gameplay loop demonstrates why Space Invaders remains a classic.
