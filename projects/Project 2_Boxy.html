BOXY – 2D Top-Down Survival Shooter

Role: Solo Developer | Designer | Programmer

Engine: Unity

Platform: PC (Web)

Play it on: Itch.io

Overview

BOXY is a 2D top-down survival shooter that places players in the role of a lone survivor stranded on a hostile planet. Armed with a variety of weapons, players must fend off relentless waves of enemies that grow stronger and more dangerous over time. The game features dynamic enemy behavior, challenging mini-boss encounters, and intense wave-based combat, all designed to test the player's skills and endurance.

Key Features

  • Diverse Arsenal: Four distinct gun types, each offering unique combat power.
  • Dynamic Enemy Behavior: Enemies become more formidable with each wave, requiring players to continuously evolve their tactics.
  • Challenging Mini-Bosses: Periodic mini-boss encounters that provide significant challenges and break the rhythm of standard waves.
  • Responsive Controls:
    • Movement: W/A/S/D keys
    • Shooting: Left Mouse Button
    • Dash: Spacebar
    • Pause: Esc key

Development Highlights

  • Solo Development: Conceptualized, designed, and developed independently, showcasing proficiency in game design, programming, and asset integration.
  • Unity Engine Utilization: Leveraged Unity's capabilities to implement real-time enemy AI behaviors, responsive controls, and dynamic combat scenarios.
  • Asset Management: urated and integrated visual and audio assets to create an immersive and cohesive game environment.

Reflection

Developing BOXY provided valuable experience in creating engaging gameplay loops and balancing difficulty progression. The project emphasized the importance of dynamic enemy design and player feedback in maintaining player engagement over extended play sessions.
